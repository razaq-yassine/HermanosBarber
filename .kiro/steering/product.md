# Product Overview

Hermanos Barber is a modern, immersive landing page for a barbershop featuring scroll-driven animations and a bold neon aesthetic.

## Core Features

- **Scroll-driven frame animation**: Hero section with 50-250 pre-rendered frames that animate based on scroll position
- **Full-screen sections**: Video sections, gallery, services, and contact information
- **Neon design system**: Black/white/yellow (#FFD700) color palette with glow effects
- **Progressive loading**: Priority frames load first, remaining frames load in background
- **Responsive design**: Optimized layouts for mobile and desktop with adaptive scroll distances

## Two Implementations

1. **HermanosBarber**: Traditional GSAP-based landing page with pinned video sections and horizontal gallery scroll
2. **HERMANOSBARBER2**: Advanced frame animation system with 50-250 frames, snap scrolling, and discrete scroll modes

## Target Audience

Professional barbershop clients seeking a modern, visually engaging web presence that showcases their work through immersive animations and high-quality media.

## Brand Identity

- Typography: Bebas Neue (headings), Montserrat (body)
- Colors: Black (#000000), White (#ffffff), Yellow (#FFD700)
- Style: Modern, bold, high-contrast with neon glow effects
- Language: French (fr)
